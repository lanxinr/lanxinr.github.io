Learning Theory What is Learning? How Learning Occurs?
Behaviorism
  • Learning is defined as "a change in observable behavior."
  • Equipotentiality: organisms learn similarly.
  • Stimulus ‚Üí Response conditioning.
  • Reinforcement (positive/negative) strengthens behavior.
  • Removing distractions to support S-R formation.
Cognitivism
  • Learning is defined as "change in mental structures(schema)."
  • Assimilation & Accommodation.
  • Information processing (attention ‚Üí encoding ‚Üí retrieval).
  • Schema construction & meaningful organization.
  • Use of prior knowledge.
Social Learning Theory
  • Learning is defined as "constructing behavior patterns society expects."
  • Through interaction with environment & people.
  • Reciprocal causation (B-E-P affect each other).
  • Modeling (observing others).
  • Vicarious reinforcement & social interaction.
